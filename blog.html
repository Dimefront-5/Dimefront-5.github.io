RE/MA Lecture Series - 3/26/24
This week, I started my lecture series on RE/MA. This will be a three-part series where I cover RE/MA basics and the tools to conduct RE/MA. This lecture series is always challenging because it is a lot of information in a short amount of time. It can be overwhelming to newcomers or people without a technical background. My goal was to try to make this as approachable as possible and something they can continue to learn more about it in their own time. I had fun trying to create my own simple labs to showcase RE/MA and creating x32 programs. With more time, I would've only used my own labs, but the PMA labs are a good starting point. If you are interested in my simple labs and lectures, check out EHC's public google drive. EHC Public Drive
